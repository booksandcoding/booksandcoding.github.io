Hey there! Want to get in touch? I'd love to hear from you! I'm open to both writing and accepting guest posts.


Send all enquiries to: bookmarkedblog@outlook.com

Want me to review your book?

That's great - I can't wait!


But first: I can't accept ebooks or self published books. Please respect this!


Am I the right person to review your book?


I don't accept:

  • Erotica
  • Romance
  • Middle grade
  • Horror

    • I do accept:

  • YA
  • Adult
  • Fantasy
  • Contemporary
  • Historical
  • Poetry

    • What should you include in your request?

    Title, author, genre, a link to your book on Goodreads/Amazon UK and why you think I would like your book. Send an email to bookmarkedblog@outlook.com.


    I post my reviews on Goodreads and my blog, and occasionally Amazon if I can be bothered. I'll review your book whether I liked it or not and always try to provide constructive criticism.


    PS - if you want me to review your book, PLEASE follow these guidelines. The people who don't are tossers who don't get their books reviewed.
